U&ME PRIVACY POLICY

We give high priority to your privacy. This Privacy Policy is prepared with an aim to describe how we gather, use protect and reveal your Personal Information, Message Data and Usage Data when you use U&Me website or WAP, any SMS, IM or other service of U&Me service such as U&Me. This Privacy Policy also elucidates your preferences for managing your data priorities. By accessing the Site or Services you consent to this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Data : We gather information that identifies you personally, like your name, mobile number and concerned personally identifiable data that you disclose to us or that you prefer to include in any U&Me account or profile (Personal Information). You may be requested to give us with definite Personal Information when you register with the Site or any of our Services and at other times. The choice to give this information is discretionary. However, if you prefer not to give the asked information, you may not be able to access certain features of the Site or Services.

Usage data : We systematically gather usage information that doesn't recognise you an individual user (Usage Data). For instance, when you download and use Services like U&Me, we automatically assimilate your device type, your Internet Service Provider, your operating system, the pages you view, your IP address and the duration and time of your site visits. We utilize this data to improve the services we provide and to help us know how people use the Site as well as Services.

Messages : When you send/receive messages with our Services like U&Me, we process and save your messages, contact data and log and other concerned details (Messaging Data) to offer these Services to you.

Cookies and Web Beacons : We make use of cookies (a small text file located on your computer system to detect your computer and browser when you see our Site or make use of our Services). Also, we use Web beacons (an electronic file located on a website to monitor usage). We never use cookies or beacons to gather your Personal Information. We need cookies and Web beacons to enhance the value of Site and Services. Majority of the Web browsers are initially organized to take cookies. You can change your Web browser settings to disable all cookies or to inform when a cookie is sent. Nevertheless, few features of the Site or Services may not work properly if you disable or delete cookies. Few of our Service Providers may use their own Web beacons and cookies in relation to the services they execute to support us.
