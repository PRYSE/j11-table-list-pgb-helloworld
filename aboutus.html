What is U&Me?

U&Me is a new age technology app for smartphone users. U&Me is conceptualized to find an alternative to regular text messaging and offer a better chatting experience to the users.

We pride ourselves on uniting people by a platform that is entertaining, engaging and safe. U&Me is loaded with new and innovative features like Free Text messaging, video/audio notes, file sharing, email attachments, location sharing, video calls, audio calls, group chat and soon. All you need is an internet connection (Wi-Fi /3G) to use U&Me app.

With U&Me you have no limits! Send any number of instant messages and call as many times you want! U&Me is a cross-platform app that works on iPhone, Android, Windows Phone and BlackBerry. Leave your old text messaging and go for U&Me for better chatting experience!

How U&Me Works

U&Me is a platform-free communication app for mobile phones that allows you to send texts without paying any charges. U&Me app is available for Android, iPhone, Windows phone and BlackBerry. U&Me uses your phone's internet data plan that you use to browse and check email, so there is no additional cost to message and get connected with your buddies.
